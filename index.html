About SwiftDrop

SwiftDrop Logistics is not just a delivery service — we're a movement toward smarter, faster, and more accountable logistics.

Born out of a need for precision, reliability, and transparency, SwiftDrop was designed to serve the dynamic needs of businesses and individuals across the Lower Mainland. Whether it’s delivering a hot meal from a local kitchen or transporting urgent packages for retail businesses, we bridge the gap between speed and trust.

Our fleet of Class 5 licensed drivers is supported by a robust tech system that offers real-time tracking, photo proof of delivery, and a streamlined quote process to make sure your delivery experience is frictionless and professional from start to finish.

From the busy streets of Vancouver to the calm neighborhoods of White Rock, from university campuses like UBC to corporate hubs in Richmond and Surrey — SwiftDrop is building the future of last-mile delivery, one package at a time.
